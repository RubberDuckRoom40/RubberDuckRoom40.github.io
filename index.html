🧭 How to Play: Text-Based Adventures
These games are interactive, story-driven experiences where you make the choices.
You'll read a scenario and then select from the options provided. Sometimes, there’s only one choice to move the story forward. But be warned:

⚠️ Wrong choices can lead to failure.
Pay close attention to the information you're given before making a decision. Success depends on what you know—not just what you click.
Ready to choose wisely?
